## CSS Object Fit

The 'object-fit' property is used to specify how an element's content should be resized to fit its container while preserving its aspect ratio.

This property is useful in situations where you are dealing with images and videos where you want to ensure that the content fits nicely within a defined space without being distorted.

Some values that are being used in 'object-fit' property are -

- fill - In this the content is resized to fill the container and this may lead to distortion in the aspect ratio.

- contain - The content is resized to fit within the container while maintaining its aspect ratio.

- cover - The content is resized to cover the entire container while maintaining its apsect ratio.

- none - The content is not resized and it maintains its original size.

- scale-down - The content is scaled down to the smallest size that fits within the container while maintaining its aspect ratio.
